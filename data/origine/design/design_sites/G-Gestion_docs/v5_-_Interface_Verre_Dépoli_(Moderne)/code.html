01. Scope of Work

This document outlines the mandatory technical requirements for the installation and maintenance of the IC-900 series industrial cooling units. The objective is to ensure thermal stability across the production floor with a variance of no more than ±0.5°C.

  • check_circle Integration with existing SCADA monitoring systems.
  • check_circle Deployment of liquid-nitrogen backup cycles.
